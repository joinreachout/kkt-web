Case study — CSC Telecom · Earlier engagement (predates current ICP focus)

CSC Telecom — customer analytics for a Travel SIM business.

Industry
Telecom — mid-sized provider
Product
Global Travel / Tourist SIM
Engagement
Data analytics studies → adopted insights
Status
Delivered

CSC Telecom asked us to improve sales and customer satisfaction for their global Travel/Tourist SIM card project. We ran a series of analytics studies across customers, regions, and products — each one shaped to land a specific decision rather than fill a report.

A number of the resulting insights were adopted by the customer and shipped into the business.

What shipped
  • Customer analytics across regions — what travelers actually do once they pick up the SIM, surfaced per geography.
  • Product mix insights — which bundles drove repeat versus one-shot use, and where the gap to top-quartile lived.
  • Adopted recommendations — multiple of our calls landed in product and marketing decisions in the next planning cycle.

Scikit-learn · AzureML · Flask · Azure
